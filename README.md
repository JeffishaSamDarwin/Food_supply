## üìò Project Overview
This project, the **"Surplus Food Distribution System,"** is an application built on the Salesforce platform. Its primary goal is to address food waste by creating a logistics network. It connects food donors (like restaurants, event venues, and hostels) with a network of NGOs and volunteers. This network can then efficiently collect and redistribute surplus food to populations in need.

The system uses core Salesforce technologies‚Äîincluding **custom objects** for the data model, **flows** for automation, and comprehensive **dashboards and reports**‚Äîto manage the entire process transparently.

---

## üöÄ Objectives
* To develop a **unified CRM platform** for managing the complete food donation lifecycle.
* To implement **automated communication** (using Salesforce Flows) between donors, NGOs, and volunteers.
* To provide **real-time tracking** for all food donations, from the moment they are logged to final delivery.
* To **decrease food surplus** and support community hunger-relief efforts through technology.
* To create **analytical dashboards and reports** that visualize key impact metrics (e.g., total food saved, people served).

---

## üß© Core Features and Modules

### **1. Donor and Partner Management**
* Onboard and manage profiles for food donors (restaurants, hostels, etc.).
* Store and maintain key details like location and contact information.
* View a complete history of donations for each donor.

### **2. Donation Lifecycle Management**
* Log new food donations with details (type, quantity, expiry).
* Trigger automatic alerts to nearby volunteers and NGOs.
* Update the donation's status in real-time (e.g., 'Pending', 'Collected', 'Delivered').

### **3. Volunteer Coordination**
* Maintain a registry of volunteers, including their contact details and service area.
* Assign tasks automatically via Salesforce flows.
* Track volunteer activity for pickups and deliveries.

### **4. NGO & Recipient Management**
* Register and manage profiles for partner NGOs.
* Allow NGOs to receive automated notifications of available food.
* Track food received by each NGO and the estimated number of beneficiaries.

### **5. Real-time Logistics Tracking**
* Create unique delivery records for each pickup.
* Update delivery status automatically using record-triggered flows.
* Provide complete end-to-end reporting on all deliveries.

### **6. Analytics and Impact Reporting**
* Display key metrics: total donations, deliveries completed, etc.
* Visualize system performance with charts and KPIs.
* Monitor the activity of all stakeholders (donors, volunteers, NGOs).
